Musivation

What is it ?

Musivation is an android app which will try to maintain your motivation during Running or Circuit training (aka "HIIT").

It's like a pact you deal with it, let me explain :

Basicly, you are choosing your number of steps/minute (for running) or your number of Work, Rest, Cycles, Times (for hiit). And, when you are in action, it will shutdown your music if you run above/under your steps or when you rest. You are its fuel ! Admit, it would be a shame not to use energy as you strive to spend !
